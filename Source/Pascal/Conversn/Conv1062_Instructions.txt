  Instructions for updating FORMIX 1.060 to 1.062
  ------------------------------------------------------

  1. Rename FORMIX.EXE to FORMIX.061
  2. From the FORMIX\FIL folder copy *.ddf to a FORMIX\FIL\backup folder.
  3. From Pervasive Control Center execute CNVF1062.SQL on the FORMIX database.
  4. From the FORMIX\TXT folder copy *.TXT to *.OLD
  5. Copy FORMIX\UPDATE\FXRPEDIT.TXT into the FORMIX\TXT folder.
  6. Copy FORMIX\UPDATE\FORMIX.EXE into the FORMIX folder.
  7. Start FORMIX.EXE
