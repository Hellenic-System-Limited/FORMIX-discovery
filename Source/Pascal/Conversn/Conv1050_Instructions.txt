  Instructions for updating FORMIX 1.049 to 1.050
  ------------------------------------------------------

  1. Rename FORMIX.EXE to FORMIX.049

  2. From the FORMIX\FIL folder copy *.ddf to a FORMIX\FIL\backup folder.
  3. From the FORMIX\FIL folder rename FXTRANS.FIL to FXTRANS.NF

  4. From Pervasive Control Center execute CNVF1050.SQL on the FORMIX database.

  5. From the FORMIX\FIL folder rename FXTRANS.NF to FXTRANS.FIL
  6. Copy FORMIX\UPDATE\FORMIX.EXE into the FORMIX folder.

  7. Start FORMIX.EXE in the normal way and yes to the creation prompt for SRCCODE.FIL
  8. From Pervasive Control Center execute CONV1050.SQL
