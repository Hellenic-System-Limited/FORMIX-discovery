  Instructions for updating FORMIX 1.062 to 1.063
  ------------------------------------------------------

  1. Rename FORMIX.EXE to FORMIX.062
  2. From the FORMIX\FIL folder copy *.ddf to a FORMIX\FIL\backup folder.
  3. From Pervasive Control Center execute CNVF1063.SQL on the FORMIX database.
  4. Copy new FORMIX.EXE into the FORMIX folder.
  5. Start FORMIX.EXE
