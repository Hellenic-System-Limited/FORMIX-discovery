Our identity
At Hellenic Systems, we’ve been driving progress in the
food and manufacturing industry since 1988. As a UK-
based and owned business, we design and manufacture
specialist computer systems that are trusted by over 130
companies across the UK and Europe.
Our identity is built on a combination of innovation and
integrity. We’re known for delivering high-performance,
adaptable software solutions that blend standardised
modules with bespoke system design — ensuring a
perfect fit for every customer. Whether supporting niche
operations or scaling across large manufacturing sites,
our systems are shaped by the specific needs of each
business we serve.
What sets us apart is our ability to combine a flexible,
forward-thinking approach with the rock-solid reliability
and performance the industry demands. We never take
a one-size-fits-all view. Instead, we draw on decades of
sector experience to develop future-ready technology
rooted in the values that have shaped us from the
very beginning.

Icon
Use the icon on its own when the area is very
small, on a profile picture or on social media
for example.
The icon can used with a 10% transparency where
appropriate across collaterals to enhance the brand.

-- 9 of 16 --

Clear Space 	Do not
Our logo needs space to allow it to stand out. Putting
the logo too close to borders, text and other assets
compromises the overall aesthetics. The minimum clear
space is derived from the width of our logo mark.
Do not reduce the logo in size below 20 mm wide
for print or 100 px for digital applications.
Do not distort or apply filters to the logo.

Our core colour palette reflects the balance between
trusted legacy and future-focused innovation - the
essence of what Hellenic Systems stands for.
Deep and dependable, our midnight blue anchors the
brand. It represents our heritage, technical expertise, and
the reliability our customers depend on. It’s a confident,
professional colour that conveys trust, precision, and
long-term performance - all essential in the food and
manufacturing sectors we serve.
The vibrant purple expresses the forward-thinking, tech-
driven side of Hellenic Systems, while the energetic pink
captures our proactive mindset and responsiveness to
customer needs. Used strategically, the pink highlights
key calls to action and moments of impact across our
communications.
Together, these colours give us a versatile and distinctive
visual language - grounded in expertise, powered by
innovation, and always human.

Hellenic Gradient
The gradient should be used as a design feature to
elevate the artwork and establish brand recognition.
The dark blue should take about 45% of the space,
the purple 35% and the pink 20%
